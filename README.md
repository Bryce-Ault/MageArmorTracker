# MageArmorTracker

A lightweight World of Warcraft Classic addon that tracks your mage armor buffs at a glance.

## Features

- Displays the icon of your active armor (Mage Armor, Ice Armor, or Frost Armor) to the left of [ResourceDisplay](https://github.com/Bryce-Ault/ResourceDisplay)
- Shows a red countdown timer when the buff has less than 5 minutes remaining
- Displays a desaturated question mark icon when no armor is applied
- Mage-only — automatically disabled for other classes

## Requirements

- [ResourceDisplay](https://github.com/Bryce-Ault/ResourceDisplay) addon

## Installation

1. Download and extract into your `Interface/AddOns/` folder
2. Ensure `ResourceDisplay` is also installed
3. Reload your UI (`/reload`)
